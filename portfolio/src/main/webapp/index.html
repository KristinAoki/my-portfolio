Kristin Aoki

Welcome and hello! I am a software engineering student at Olin College of Engineering. When the weather is warm and sunny you can find me hiking or on a golf course. When the weather isn't so nice, I will be found wrapped in a blanket reading a book or watching a romantic comedy. I also enjoy spending my time writing letters to friends and family members. As a result, I have recently picked up the art of calligraphy.

I grew up in San Antonio, Texas and went to North East School of the Arts (NESA). At NESA I dedicated many hours to the orchestra, playing the viola for many musicals and concerts. I decided to focus on fine arts in high school instead of tech/engineering because I knew it would be the last time I could really engage with this passion.

I decided to go into the field of engineering because engineers have a solid hand in the future of the world. Without engineering we would be a very rudimentary society. I want to use my knowledge to empower people and create a better society. I am still unsure how I will accomplish this goal, but it is something I strive towards every day.

  • FollowBot - Designed and built a robot that follows a person. The robots was primarily controlled by an Arduino. The computer vision used to follow a person was run on a Raspberry Pi.
